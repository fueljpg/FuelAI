Features

Fuel AI tracks Nutrition, Training, Sleep together—and uses the relationships to coach you better than single-purpose apps.

AI Calorie Scanner

Barcode + label scanning with on-device AI to estimate calories/macros fast. Learns your meals for one-tap logging.

Macro Targets

Goal-based macros (cut, maintenance, bulk) that adapt to activity and recovery.

Gym Fitness Planner

Your Gym AI builds periodized plans (strength, hypertrophy, endurance) from your history and equipment.

Gym Tracker

Auto-log sets, reps, weight, RIR/RPE, and PRs. Timer + rest suggestions based on your goal.

Sleep Sync

Connect smartwatches to import sleep stages, HR, and variability into a simple recovery score.

Progress in 3 Fields

One dashboard for Nutrition × Training × Sleep. See correlations and trends, not just isolated metrics.

Better than the competition

Instead of 3 separate apps with disconnected data, Fuel AI unifies calories, workouts, and sleep—then coaches across them.

Adaptive Coaching

Poor sleep? Training load tapers and calories rebalance. Great recovery? Push volume and protein up.

Privacy first

Local preferences, export anytime, and a straightforward data policy.
