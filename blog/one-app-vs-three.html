One App vs Three: Why Consistency Wins

Most lifters bounce between a calorie tracker, a gym log, and a sleep app. That creates friction and lost context. Fuel AI tracks the three pillars together—and coaches the interaction between them.

Unified progress

Nutrition adherence, training volume, and sleep quality roll into a single weekly score. You’ll see trends like “late sessions + low sleep → missed macros,” and you’ll get specific fixes.
