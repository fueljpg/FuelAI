One app for Nutrition, Training, and Sleep.

Fuel AI combines an AI Calorie Scanner, a Gym Planner + Tracker, and Sleep Sync from your smartwatch. One timeline. One progress score. Less friction—better results.

AI Calorie Scanner

Snap a barcode or label—Fuel AI estimates calories & macros and learns your favorites. No more 20-tap entries.

Gym Planner & Tracker

Your Gym AI builds evidence-based plans for your goal and auto-logs sets, reps, RPE, and PRs—so you lift, not fiddle.

Sleep & Recovery

Connect your smartwatch to sync sleep, HR, and recovery. Fuel AI adjusts calories and training load based on readiness.

Why Fuel AI beats juggling three apps

MyFitnessPal tracks calories. Strong tracks lifts. Sleep apps track sleep. Fuel AI tracks the relationship between them—and coaches you through it.

  1. 1
    Unified progress. One dashboard shows Nutrition × Training × Sleep and how each affects the others.
  2. 2
    Adaptive coaching. Better sleep? Fuel AI nudges volume up. Tough day? It dials intensity down and rebalances calories.
  3. 3
    Lower friction. Faster logging + smarter defaults = higher consistency = better outcomes.
